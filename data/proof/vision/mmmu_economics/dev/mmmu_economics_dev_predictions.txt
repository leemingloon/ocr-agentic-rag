**Question:** What is the opportunity cost of producing **1 unit of Glasses in Mexico**?

**Method:** Opportunity cost = What you give up / What you get

In Mexico:
- 1 unit of Glasses requires **12 workers**

- With 12 workers, Mexico could produce: 12/10 = **1.20 units of Shoes**
