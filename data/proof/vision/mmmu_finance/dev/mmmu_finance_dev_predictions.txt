**Formula for Present Value of Ordinary Annuity:**

- r = 10% = 0.10
- n = 5 years

$$PV = 22,500 \times \frac{1-(1.10)^{-5}}{0.10}$$

$$(1.10)^5 = 1.61051$$

$$1 - \frac{1}{1.61051} = 1 - 0.62092 = 0.37908$$

$$\frac{0.37908}{0.10} = 3.79079$$

$$PV = 22,500 \times 3.79079 = \mathbf{\$85,293}$$
