$$\sigma_p = \sqrt{(0.50)^2(0)^2 + (0.50)^2(14)^2 + 0}$$
$$\sigma_p = \sqrt{0 + (0.25)(196)}$$
$$\sigma_p = \sqrt{49} = 7\%$$
